Why visit Yogyakarta by Tuk Tuk?

What better way is there than touring in a tuktuk with a local driver and guide who can tell you everything you want to know?

Yogyakarta is famous as the center of Javanese celebrated classical arts and culture, such as batik, silversmithing, and traditional dance. Still ruled by a monarchy you can visit the king's palace within the city center. Not only that, but it's located in the middle of the must-see temple complexes of Borobudur and Prambanan.

With so much to see and do going with a guided tuktuk tour will give you an extra insight into Javanese life and culture.

About Us

We have a whole variety of tours from visting markets, historic temples to traditional villages as well as tours where you can learn about and experience traditional forms of art alongside the latest in street art. We have been running our tours for several years and have a wealth of knowledge about the history and culture of the area.

Our guides are here to give you an unforgettable experience. They are all local, speak excellent English and will help you with any souvenir shopping, or choosing the best things to try in the markets and street food stalls!
